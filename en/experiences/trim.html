Trim

Company: TRIM Srl, Turin IT

Trim is a Turin based company focusing on the design and development of online applications and systems for documents and data base management solutions mainly developed using Java technology. Made of a team of 15 engineers it develops management systems for a wide range of uses: logistic, budgeting, human resources management, intranet portals, work flow management and much more. My experience in Trim as intern has been focused on the design and development of a CMS platform based on Doqui and the Semanitc Web rules and technologies which I’ve also introduced as a case study during my engineer final dissertation. RDF(S), Triple(N3), SPARQL, ARC2.
